Information We Collect

At The B & K Bakery, we are committed to being transparent about how we collect and use information. Here are the types of information we gather:

Information You Provide Directly

When you interact with us through our website, contact forms, or when placing an order, we collect the following personal information:

  • Contact Information: Your full name, email address, and phone number
  • Delivery Information: Your complete mailing address (currently limited to California)
  • Order Details: Specific preferences, dietary restrictions, customization requests, and special occasion information
  • Payment Information: PayPal email address

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Browser Information: Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, time spent on our site, links clicked, and referral source
  • IP Address: Your internet protocol address (used for security and analytics)
  • Cookies & Session Data: To enhance your browsing experience and remember your preferences

Information About Your Orders

To fulfill your baked goods orders effectively, we maintain:

  • Order history and previous selections
  • Preparation timeline and status updates
  • Delivery or pickup details
  • Special requests and ingredient preferences

These will be communicated through your email address

💡 Important Note: We only collect information necessary to serve you better. We never require unnecessary personal data to browse our website or view our menu.

Client-side storage (LocalStorage)

To provide a smooth shopping and checkout experience we temporarily store a small set of data in your browser's localStorage. The keys we use are:

  • bk_cart — the items in your basket (product id, name, price, quantity, optional image).
  • bk_checkout — checkout form values you enter (name, phone, email, delivery address, requested date/time, special instructions). This is used to prefill the checkout form and is not shared publicly.
  • bk_last_order — a record of the most recent order placed in this browser (order id, items, totals, and PayPal transaction id). This is cleared from your browser after you view the order confirmation page.

Payment data: We do not store raw payment card numbers, CVV codes, or other sensitive payment data. Payments are processed by PayPal; card details are entered into PayPal-managed secure fields and transmitted directly to PayPal. We never write card details to localStorage or to our servers.

Order confirmations & emails: Order confirmation emails are sent by our server-side email service after a successful payment. We do not include raw payment card data in email communications.

Retention & deletion: Local data described above is removed from your browser after a successful checkout. You can also clear your browser storage at any time to remove these items.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to enhance your experience with The B & K Bakery and ensure we serve you delicious, customized baked goods. Here's how we use the data you provide:

Order Fulfillment & Delivery

  • Process and prepare your orders with proper attention to special requests
  • Confirm order details and prepare timeline (2 days preparation required)
  • Arrange delivery or schedule pickup appointments
  • Contact you with order updates and delivery confirmations
  • Send payment invoices and receipts

Communication & Customer Service

  • Respond to your inquiries and customer support requests
  • Send order confirmations and status updates
  • Address any concerns or issues with orders
  • Request feedback on your experience

What We Do NOT Do With Your Information

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties
  • We do not share your email address for marketing purposes without consent
  • We do not use cookies for tracking outside our website
  • We do not retain payment card information on our servers

Our Commitment: Your information helps us craft handmade baked goods with care and precision. Every detail you share helps us create the perfect order for you.

Order & Contact Form Handling

Our order and contact forms are designed to be simple and secure. Here's how we handle the information you submit:

Order Form Data

When you submit an order through our website, we collect:

  • Your name and contact details
  • Delivery address (must be within California)
  • Order specifications and customization requests
  • Preferred delivery or pickup date (minimum 2 days from order date)
  • Any special dietary requirements or allergies

Data Security & Encryption

  • All form submissions are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Sensitive information is protected with industry-standard security measures
  • Payment processing is handled by PCI-DSS compliant third-party providers
  • We do not store complete credit card information on our servers

Service Area Limitation

The B & K Bakery currently operates within California only. When placing orders, delivery addresses must be within California state boundaries. If you provide a non-California address, we will contact you to clarify or cancel the order.

Verification: We may verify your phone number and address to ensure accurate delivery of your order.

Email Communications

Email is one of our primary ways to keep you updated about your orders and maintain connection with The B & K Bakery.

Types of Emails You'll Receive

  • Transactional Emails: Order confirmations, preparation updates, delivery notifications, and receipts

Managing Your Email Preferences

Email Frequency

  • Order updates: As needed for your specific order

Third-Party Services & Partners

To serve you better, we work with trusted partners and service providers. Here's how your information is handled:

Payment Processing

Your payment information is processed by PayPal. PayPal:

  • Meet PCI-DSS compliance standards for payment security
  • Do not share payment information with us
  • Have their own privacy policies governing data handling
  • Are contractually obligated to protect your financial information

Delivery & Logistics Partners

If you choose delivery, we may share your address with local delivery services only for:

  • Fulfilling your order delivery
  • Tracking and confirming delivery status
  • These partners maintain confidentiality agreements with us

Our Data Sharing Principles

  • We only share data when necessary to fulfill your order or service
  • All partners are contractually obligated to protect your privacy
  • You will always know if data is shared with third parties
  • We never sell customer information to unrelated businesses

Partner Vetting: We carefully vet all service partners to ensure they meet our privacy and security standards.

Data Protection & Security

Your privacy and security are paramount to us. We implement comprehensive measures to protect your information.

Security Measures

  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transmitted to and from our website is encrypted
  • Secure Servers: Our servers are protected with firewalls and intrusion detection
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access customer information
  • Regular Updates: We maintain current security patches and software updates
  • Backup Systems: Your data is regularly backed up to secure locations

Data Breaches

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we are committed to:

  • Notifying affected individuals promptly
  • Working with authorities to investigate
  • Taking steps to prevent future incidents
  • Providing guidance on protecting your personal information

International Data Transfer

As a California-based bakery, The B & K Bakery complies with U.S. data protection laws and California's consumer privacy regulations. Your information is not transferred internationally.

🛡️ Continuous Improvement: We regularly review and update our security practices to stay ahead of emerging threats.

Your Privacy Rights

We believe data privacy is a fundamental right. Here are the rights you have regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of all personal information we hold about you:

  • Request your data in a readable format
  • Receive information about how we use your data
  • Understand who has access to your information

Right to Correction

You have the right to correct inaccurate information:

  • Update your contact information anytime
  • Correct order preferences or delivery details
  • Fix any errors in your account information

Right to Deletion

You can request that we delete your information (subject to legal requirements):

  • Request deletion of marketing records
  • Ask us to remove your account entirely
  • Note: We may retain order records for tax purposes (up to 2 years)
  • After deletion, we cannot restore the information

Right to Opt-Out

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Request not to receive promotional communications
  • You'll still receive transactional order updates

Right to Data Portability

You can request that we transfer your data to another service:

  • Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Use your information with other service providers

Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you have the right to:

  • Contact us directly to resolve the issue
  • Lodge a complaint with California privacy authorities
  • Seek legal recourse if necessary
